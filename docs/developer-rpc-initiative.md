# Developer RPC Initiative

Star Atlas is committed to empowering community developers. Through our *Developer RPC Initiative* we cover the infrastructure costs of Solana RPC calls so you can focus on building great tools, analytics, and gameplay extensions for the ecosystem.

## What the program provides

* **Free, production-grade RPC endpoints** for Star Atlas projects

* **Best-practice guides** and tutorials on data access and RPC usage, published on [build.staratlas.com](/)

* **Example code** in the open-source cookbook ([github.com/staratlasmeta/star-atlas-cookbook](https://github.com/staratlasmeta/star-atlas-cookbook)). Community pull requests are welcome.

> **Eligibility:** your project must be **open-source or offer open public access** to qualify for a free RPC key.

## How to apply

1. Make sure your repo or hosted app is public.
2. Fill out this short form: <https://forms.gle/oRfGsuc1ArPSj4NBA>
3. Our infra team will review and email you the credentials.

By lowering operating costs and sharing examples, we aim to foster a strong, collaborative developer community around Star Atlas. If you have questions or ideas, reach out in the [#community-developers Discord channel](https://discord.com/channels/789226204980707409/1004095538544787547).

Let’s build together!
