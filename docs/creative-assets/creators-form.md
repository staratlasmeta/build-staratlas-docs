# Creators Form

After you have reviewed the [**Star Atlas Terms of Service**](https://staratlas.com/terms-of-service) closely and developed an idea of your own, you will need to fill out the [**User Services Release Form**](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1koPfnGWW06AF9z09lHjtI1f19ZZy9cLF4OgnKI24S4w/viewform?ts=684aeba0\&edit_requested=true) to provide us with some basic information about yourself and your product or service.

If you have any questions about filling out the form, please let us know on the [**Star Atlas Discord**](https://discord.gg/staratlas).
