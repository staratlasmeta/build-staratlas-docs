# Star Atlas Assets

Looking for visuals, logos, or in-game artwork to power your Star Atlas creation?\
You’ll find everything we’ve made available for public use in the [Star Atlas Canon Asset Repository](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1QDkyrcd1LiDzEAK_9bIUg6V1VM9-U8Sl)

Everything in that folder is approved for use under the Terms of Service\
If it’s not in the folder, it’s not approved for use (yet)

### What’s Inside the Asset Library

#### Artwork

* Characters
* Factions (including character leaders)
* Land and Claim Stakes
* Posters and Collectibles
* Resources
* Ships

#### Game Art

* WebGL assets from SAGE
* Ships sorted by brand: Armstrong, Busan, Calico, Fimbul, Ogrika and more

#### **Logos**

* Faction Logos
* Manufacturer Logos
* Never Alone campaign assets (legacy)
* “Star Atlas: Powered by the People” logo

#### What’s Not Included

To keep things clean and legally safe, we don’t include:

* Unreleased or in-progress content
* Internal design docs or drafts
* The official “Star Atlas” logo or stylized wordmark (these are restricted trademarks)
* Trade secrets or proprietary elements<br>
