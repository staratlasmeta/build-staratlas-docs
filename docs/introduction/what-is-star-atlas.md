# What is Star Atlas?

Star Atlas is a next-gen sci-fi universe powered by the Solana blockchain, part MMO, part strategy game, part open economy.

It’s composed of several live and evolving products, this is what iswe have live today:

### [PLAY](https://play.staratlas.com/)

Your main Star Atlas hub ([play.staratlas.com](https://play.staratlas.com/))

* Manage your ships, crew, DAC (guild), and NFT inventory
* Enlist in Faction Fleet or Claim Stakes to earn passive rewards
* Buy, sell, and trade assets on the Galactic Marketplace
* Rent fleets or earn from yours with Fleet Rental<br>

### [SAGE](https://based.staratlas.com/)

A 4X browser-based game, fully on-chain

* Mine, craft, and transport resources
* Compete in faction-based economic strategy
* All gameplay actions are signed and settled on Solana
* Built for modular expansion by builders<br>

### [DAO](https://govern.staratlas.com/)

Shape the future of the metaverse

* Lock POLIS to gain voting power (PVP)
* Vote on proposals, guide treasury usage, and ecosystem growth
* Govern key parts of the Star Atlas economy

### [UE5 Game](https://store.epicgames.com/fr/p/star-atlas-bead34)

A high-fidelity Unreal Engine 5 experience (Epic Games)

* Fly your ships, explore space, race, and battle in multiplayer
* Use your Crew NFTs as avatars
* Mine asteroids and experience evolving gameplay modes
* Fully free-to-play, with optional NFT integration\ <br>

### For Builders

Star Atlas isn’t just a game,  it’s a permissionless platform.\
Build tools, games, lore, assets, and businesses inside a decentralized, community-owned sci-fi metaverse and find immediate exposure to a large, committed, and growing community.
